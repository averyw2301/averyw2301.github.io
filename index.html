Interesting facts about music

Music can help plants grow: A study found that playing classical music to rice plants helped them grow faster. Music can improve your mood and excercise: Listening to music while working out can help you run faster and longer. Music can improve your health: Music can lower blood pressure, reduce stress, and increase serotonin and endorphins. Music can improve your focus: Music can help you concentrate and be more productive. Music can change your perception: Music can alter the way you see the world. The oldest instrument is the flute.
