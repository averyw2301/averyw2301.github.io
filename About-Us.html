About Us

Our Mission

At Players Club Management, we are dedicated to empowering student-athletes by maximizing their Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) opportunities. Our mission is to help athletes build their personal brands, secure meaningful partnerships, and create long-term financial success.

Our Story

Players Club Management was founded with a passion for sports and a vision to help athletes navigate the evolving NIL landscape. Our goal is to ensure that student-athletes are fairly compensated and educated on financial literacy, branding, and career planning.
